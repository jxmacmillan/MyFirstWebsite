About Me

Early Years

I was born in the early 1970s in a small town in upstate New York. My parents were both artists, and I grew up surrounded by their paintings and sculptures. I loved going to art museums and watching my father work in his studio. I started painting and drawing when I was very young. I loved mixing different colors together and seeing how they changed. I also loved creating characters and stories. I went to art school in Boston, and then moved to New York City after graduation. I started my own studio and began painting and illustrating children's books. Recent Years I now live in Los Angeles with my husband and two young children. I continue to paint and illustrate children's books, and I also teach art classes to kids. I love spending time with my family and exploring new places.

Waldorf School of Baltimore

I am a Waldorf School of Baltimore alumna and current parent. I am passionate about providing a Waldorf education for my children and am involved in the school community in many ways.

Deforestation

Deforestation is the removal of forests, by whatever means. Deforestation occurs for a variety of reasons: logging for timber, fuel, or other purposes clearing land for agriculture or other development fires natural disasters, such as hurricanes and floods Deforestation can have a number of negative consequences, including: loss of habitat for animals destruction of watersheds erosion increased global warming loss of oxygen poisoning of soil and water

Use of Child Labor

My name is Darlene, and I am a concerned citizen who is deeply troubled by the use of child labor in developing countries. I believe that all children have the right to a quality education and safe working conditions, and I am committed to raising awareness about this issue. Every day, millions of children are forced to work in dangerous and abusive conditions, often for little or no pay. These children are denied their childhoods, and they often miss out on crucial education and developmental opportunities. The use of child labor is a serious human rights violation, and it must be addressed urgently. I urge everyone to learn more about this issue and to take action to end child labor. Thank you for your commitment to this important cause.
